Our Story

From Kovilpatti to Every Corner of South India

Founded in 2012 by Sri Rathinasamy Jayaprakash, Centum Nutrifoods is a remarkable story of passion and perseverance. At the age of 66, our visionary promoter brought to life a dream of making traditional Peanut Chikki (Kadalai Mittai) from Kovilpatti — often hailed as the Lonavala of South India — accessible to all.

Now nearing 80, Mr. Jayaprakash continues to inspire with his unwavering commitment to quality and excellence. Thanks to strict quality control, authentic recipes, and exceptional after-sales service, CENTUM products have found their way into over 500+ premium retail outlets across Tamil Nadu and Kerala.
